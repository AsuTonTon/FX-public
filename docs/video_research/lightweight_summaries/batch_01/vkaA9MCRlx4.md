## 3. Setup and context

The method waits for a golden cross for long-side third-wave attempts or a dead cross for short-side third-wave attempts, then waits for the first 20EMA touch after that cross. ## 4. Entry condition

The entry condition starts by waiting for a 20EMA touch after the relevant EMA cross. The touch may be made by the candle body or wick. That touch candle becomes the reference candle. For a long entry, the reference candle high must be broken and confirmed by candle body close. For a short entry, the reference candle low must be broken and confirmed by candle body close. A wick-only break is insufficient. A skip condition is triggered if the opposite EMA cross appears before confirmation. Immediate touch entries are avoided as damashi risk, and later touches after the same cross are not valid for this method.
