Beginners are directed back to coherent practice, risk management, and expectancy awareness, while recognizing that simple expectancy arithmetic needs actual trade evidence before implementation. |

Taken together, the playlist reads as a beginner progression from structure
recognition, to advanced structure caution, to timeframe discipline, to
survival-first risk control, and finally to process discipline.
